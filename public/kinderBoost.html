Activities and Events

Activities are the best route of academic success. Activities play a vital role in children's education. Since, preschool activities enhance the quality of learning, they are important.

It helps them to improve cognitive skills, build confidence, develop social skills, fine motor skills. Basically preschool activities and aids encourage children to think rationally. KINDER BOOST PRESCHOOL has designed the activities in such a way that every child will develop his or her physical as well as cognitive skills through the activities.

Parent Sessions

Planning a parents session provides parents an opportunity to promote positive discipline and behaviour between parents and their children.

Involvement in session benefits in creating a more supportive learning environment, which leads to better academic and overall performance also.

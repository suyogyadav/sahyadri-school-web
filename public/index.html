About Us

At our institute, we transcend the traditional notion of a school. It's a captivating realm where students and teachers alike embark on a journey of shared learning. Our approach extends beyond conventional textbooks, classrooms, and chalkboards. We've crafted a comprehensive learning system with the objective of nurturing the complete personality of each child, providing exposure to a diverse array of learning experiences.

In this dynamic learning environment, we prioritize fostering a sense of curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity. Our institute is not just a place for academic pursuits; it's a vibrant community that encourages exploration and collaboration. Through innovative teaching methods and interactive learning tools, we empower our students to become active participants in their education.

Facilities

Science lab

Science Laboratory at Sahyadri National School is well equipped with all the necessary apparatus. It is completely a safe place for students to carry out various experiments and build young budding scientists.

Computer Lab

In this Era of Information Technology we have a well equipped computer lab set up. Spacious laboratory with 35+ computers for students to work individually. With all latest licensed versions,we also train students for coding which is one of the NEP highlights.

Book Treasure

A spacious and dedicated place for children to nurture their reading habits. It has variety of books for students of Std 1 to 10 in English, Marathi and Hindi. A huge store of over 2000 books is the specialty of the Library at Sahyadri National School.

E-learning

To create and facilitate learning experiences we have 40 + classrooms equipped with digital boards.

Activity hall

Multipurpose Halls We are proud to have has 4 spacious multipurpose halls one on each floor for conducting various activities,competitions and meetings.

In House Couching

We at Sahyadri National School give lot of importance to physical fitness for building a healthy citizen for our Nation. Inhouse Coaching is given to the students after the school hours for Taekwando, Wrestling, Kabaddi, Kho- kho and Judo. Our students have been winners not only at district bu international level as well.

Sankul

Sahyadri Kusti Sankul a training centre for the wrestling sports is the brainchild of our Founder Chairman Honourable Vijay Barate Sir. Every year more than 150 students are trained and many have brought laurels at International level too.
